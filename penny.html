About Us

A highly secure wallet is a must for anyone who holds digital assets like cryptocurrency. Pennyswap wallet allows you store your assets using Advanced Cryptography secured system to secure your private keys. Our mission is to create a world where we bridge the gap between asset security and user experience by enabling traders and investors interact with various blockchans with ease and optimised user experience.

Users are key in everything we do. We are committed to building a safe , secure and easy to use product while abiding strictly to regulatory standards. This is to maintain integrity to bolster support to further build better products in the future.
